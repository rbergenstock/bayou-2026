📊 THE QUALIFICATION MATH

How regional qualification works:
Teams earn points from: Qualification Performance + Alliance Selection + Playoff Advancement + Awards + Team Age.
Your two best events count. Top ~50-60 of ~1,630 regional teams get invited.
The cutline is typically around 130-140 combined points from 2 events.

Shortcut: Win the event = automatic qualification, regardless of points.
