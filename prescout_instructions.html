Bayou Regional · April 1–4, 2026 · Pontchartrain Center, Kenner, LA · 54 Teams

📋 WHAT IS THIS?

The Bayou Pre-Scout Viewer is an intelligence report on all 54 teams attending the Bayou Regional. It pulls data from The Blue Alliance and Statbotics to show you OPR ratings, EPA ratings, prior event results, win/loss records, and scouting intel — all in one place.

Your job: learn the field before we get there.

🚀 GETTING STARTED

1
Download bayou_prescout_v2.html from the Slack scouting channel
2
Open it in Chrome, Edge, or any browser — works on phone, Chromebook, laptop, or tablet. No internet needed after download.
3
You'll see all 54 teams organized by tier with color-coded cards

🎯 YOUR PRE-SCOUT ASSIGNMENT

1
Browse the cards and pick a team you want to scout. Look for teams that interest you or that nobody else has claimed yet.
2
Click the card to go to that team's page on The Blue Alliance. Look for their 2026 matches with video (📺 icons).
3
Watch 2-3 qualification matches. Focus on: What do they score? How do they move? Do they play defense? Can they climb? Do they break down?
4
Click the green 🎯 SCOUT A TEAM button to open the Google Form. Select the team from the dropdown, fill in what you observed, and submit.
5
You're done. That team is now claimed — it will disappear from the dropdown so nobody else doubles up on it.
⏱️ Time estimate: 15-20 minutes per team. Watch the matches, fill the form, done. We have 54 teams to cover — if everyone grabs 2-3 teams, we'll know the entire field before we get to Kenner.
